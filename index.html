Law

Trafalgar Law is the Captain of the Heart Pirates and one of the most fearsome pirates in One piece. He can generate a blade of energy that can use to damage his enemies internal organs without leaving an external wound.

The Worst Generation


The Worst Generation refers to a special generation of pirates who arrived at Sabaody Archipelago with a bounty of over 100 million berries.

Allies


Luffy, Kid and Law joined forces back in Sabaody Archipelago to beat the navy, they are the 3 strongest Supernovas and after the event in Sabaody Archipelago.

Devil Fruit

Law ate the Ope Ope no Mi, a Paramecia type Devil Fruit allows him to create a sphere of light blue aura, inside which he can manipulate the fabric of space. He use Shambles by creating ‘room’ within the space and switch places of any object inside.

Haki Power

Law has shown great proficiency in Busoshoku Haki, is a form of Haki that allows the user to use their own spiritual energy to create, in essence, an invisible armor around themself, providing incredible offensive and defensive capabilities.

Devil Fruit

Luffy's rubberiness is a result of eating a Devil Fruit called the Gomu Gomu no Mi, which makes him immune to electric attacks and most blunt forces also gives him the ability to stretch his body.

Haki Power

Luffy possesses the power to use all three types of Haki (Kenbunshoku, Busoshoku, Haoshoku). In fact, Luffy has successfully taken his Armament and Observation Haki to extreme levels, making him a magnificent pirate.

Devil Fruit

Kid using his magnetism to form a giant hand out of scrap metal. Kid has eaten a currently unnamed Paramecia-type Devil Fruit that allows him to manipulate magnetic forces.

Haki Power

Esutass Kid use Haoshoku Haki is a rare form of Haki that allows the user to exert their own willpower over others. It is said that whoever possesses this type of Haki has the qualities of a king, and are known asHao literally means "Supreme King/Ruler”.

Heart Pirates Crew Members


The Heart Pirates are an infamous and notable rookie pirate crew from the North Blue and introduced on Sabaody Archipelago around the time the Straw Hat Pirates arrived there two years ago. Their captain, Trafalgar D. Water Law, is a member of the Worst Generation and a former member of the Seven Warlords of the Sea.
